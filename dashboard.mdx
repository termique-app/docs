The Dashboard is the first thing you see after unlocking Termique. It gives you an overview of your SSH connections and a way to jump straight into one.

## Stats

Four counters at the top: **Hosts**, **Active Sessions**, **Groups**, and **Snippets**: a quick read on the size of your setup and how many sessions are currently connected.

## Recent hosts

Your 6 most recently updated hosts, each with a **Connect** button that opens a terminal session directly from the Dashboard. Hosts shared with you are marked with a **Shared** badge.

## All hosts

Below Recent hosts is the full list, with a search box (matches label, hostname, or username) and tag filtering, the same filtering available on the [Hosts](/hosts) page.

## Add a host

Click **Add Host** in the top right, or from the empty state if you have none yet. See [Hosts](/hosts) for the fields.
