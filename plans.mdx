Termique has a Free plan and a Pro subscription. Free covers solo use on a handful of hosts; Pro removes the caps and unlocks team features.

## Free vs Pro

| Feature | Free | Pro |
|---------|------|-----|
| Hosts | Up to 3 | Unlimited |
| Snippets | Up to 10 | Unlimited |
| Sharing hosts with teammates | Not available | Available |
| Command logs | Not available | Available |
| AI assistant (hosted) | 15 prompts/day | 100 prompts/day |
| AI memory | Not available | Available |
| Monitoring — background push alerts | Not available | Available |
| Monitoring history | 5 min: 7 days · 1 hr: 30 days · 1 day: 90 days | 5 min: 30 days · 1 hr: 1 year · 1 day: 3 years |

<Note>
  A custom (BYO) AI endpoint is not metered by Termique regardless of plan — see [AI assistant](/ai-assistant).
</Note>

## Upgrade

Open **Settings → Subscription** and click **Upgrade**. Payment is handled by Polar; you'll be redirected to a secure checkout.

## Manage your subscription

Open **Settings → Subscription** and click **Manage subscription** to open the Polar customer portal. From there you can update payment details, view invoices, or cancel.

If you cancel, Pro features stay active until the end of your current billing period.

## Hitting a limit

If you're on Free and hit a cap (e.g. a 4th host, or an 11th snippet), Termique shows an upgrade prompt instead of completing the action. Nothing is deleted or downgraded automatically — remove an existing item or upgrade to continue.
